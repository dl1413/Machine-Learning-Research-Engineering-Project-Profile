## Brief Description of Duties
The Research Support Specialist works under the supervision of Principal Investigator
Dr. Shelby McClelland in the School of Marine and Atmospheric Sciences and will
assist with assessing changing cropland use and management in Bangladesh for
industrial animal agriculture. The position requires the ability to work
independently as well as part of the team with minimal oversight. Strong
problem-solving abilities and attention to detail are essential. The successful
incumbent will have strong oral and written communication skills.

### Responsibilities
- Assist in conducting a literature review, including peer-reviewed publications and national agricultural censuses, to assess changing cropland use and management in Bangladesh for industrial animal agriculture (for review by PI)
- Harmonize different data streams — geospatial products, land-use and crop management data, national food supply utilization, livestock densities, etc. — following established protocols (for review by PI)
- Create novel data products utilizing data analysis, interpretation, and visualization (for review by PI)
- Assist with drafting and writing project reports and any peer-reviewed manuscripts (for review by PI)
- Other duties as assigned
