Use these for cold outreach (connect notes), warm intros (referral asks), and recruiter follow-ups. All use your **3 primary projects** as proof points. Keep the connection-note variants under LinkedIn's **300-character** free-tier limit (~50 words).

## Quick rules

1. **Never copy a message verbatim.** The first sentence has to reference something specific to the recipient — a paper, a product, a blog post, a recent talk. If you can't find a real reference in 5 minutes, skip the message.
2. **One ask per message.** Connect, OR intro, OR meeting — never two.
3. **Follow up at most once.** If they don't respond after a single bump, move on.
4. **Don't apologize for reaching out.** Don't open with "Sorry to bother you" or "I know you're busy." It's a signal that you don't believe you belong in the conversation.
